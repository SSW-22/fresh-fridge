# Fresh Fridge

## Description

Fresh Fridge is a user-friendly website that helps individuals keep track of their kitchen inventory, create grocery lists, and search for recipes all in one place. 

## Live


## Key Features and Functionalities

### Inventory list
The inventory list feature of Fresh Fridge allows you to keep track of all the ingredients in your kitchen.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929672-d6c25487-c467-4622-8216-4605ff3a204b.png" width="300" height="500">

The inventory list feature also provides an at-a-glance view of what you have on hand, helping you plan meals and grocery trips more efficiently.
Fresh Fridge automatically highlights items that are close to expiring.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929736-374ad83f-c6f4-4d17-8b32-f4276563ddb7.png" width="300" height="500">

Easily add items to inventory list by inputting their name, quantity, location, and expiry date. Once you've entered the information, simply save it to your inventory list.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929747-0bdb1e6d-4269-4d76-81a5-1fbff5fd2a74.png" width="300" height="500">

The inventory list can be sorted and viewed by location, such as fridge, freezer, or pantry, allowing you to quickly find the items you need.


### Grocery list

Fresh Fridge also includes a grocery list feature that allows you to create a list of items you need to purchase. 

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929766-72669d61-d045-4934-aeea-ce8252d1aabd.png" width="300" height="500"> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929769-70a8c96d-cf74-460c-af72-b7960388279c.png" width="300" height="500">

You can add items to your grocery list manually. Once you purchase an item on your grocery list, you can easily transfer it to your inventory list to keep track of your supplies.


### Recipe search

Fresh Fridge also includes a recipe search feature that allows you to search for and save your favorite recipes.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929786-be0900bb-67ed-404c-a2cb-bb4d546e9f25.png" width="300" height="500">

Simply enter the name of the dish or the main ingredient, and Fresh Fridge will provide you with a list of relevant recipes. You can save your favorite recipes to your recipe collection for easy access later.

### Other features

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/59901905/231929801-afff0b9e-8ab2-4d71-91d9-1cec92f791ad.png" width="300" height="500">

Fresh Fridge also includes a notification feature that allows you to set a notification period. Simply input the desired notification period on the profile page, and Fresh Fridge will notify you when the expiry date is approaching or has passed.
